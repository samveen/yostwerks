After the glue is dry, overlap the areas to be glued and apply tape to hold them in place. Use a heat gun to reactivate the glue and apply hand pressure to assure a good bond. Remove the tape in a few minutes. if the tape sticks, apply a little heat while peeling back the tape. At the area where the keel meets the stem, the small skin overlap should be patched.
