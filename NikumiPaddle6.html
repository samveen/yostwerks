The non-folding wood frame "Nikumi" is a multi-chine Baidarka. The frame is a combination of cedar stringers and plywood cross sections and coaming. The skin is made with clear vinyl. The frame is finished in Watco Danish oil. The next several pages display various frame construction pics ( Click "Next Page"). In addition, The Nikumi and Nikumi 19 Offsets and several additional pages of construction pics are at the end of this section. Use the (BACK) key to return. Either Nikumi can also be constructed using traditional methods, or as an aluminum frame folder.
