A small kayak like the 15ft (4.57m) Sea Otter has a packed size of 42in (106cm) and is ideal for mototrcycle transport. The travel bag used is a PVC dry bag that has been extended with an additional 10in PVC section. In addition to the kayak, the bag contains a paddle, sea sock, spray skirt, and PFD. Additional kayaking gear is carried in the "Givi" trunk bag. Several pages of details can be found in the Motorcycle / Folding Kayak Link. Use the (BACK) key to return.
