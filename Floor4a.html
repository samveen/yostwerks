Cutting Cedar Slats

The Sea Ranger floor is made from 2 X 4 fence cedar. The slats are cut using a table saw or circular saw with the fence set at 3/8".

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Sanding Slats

A palm sander with 80 or 100 grit sandpaper is used to smooth out the surfaces.
