Rich, from British Columbia, Canada displays his Nikumi baidarka. The cross sections are 5/8" thick birch ply with WRC stringers. The Skin is 8oz nylon with Varathane Colours in Plastic enamel. His main paddle is laminated WRC & Yellow Cedar, storm paddle is single piece WRC. The boat weighs 36 lbs.
