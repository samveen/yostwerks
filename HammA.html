Bill Hamm from Machesney Park, IL. supervised the building of this multi-chine Sea Bee at a kayak building class. The wood frame is skinned with dacron and fiberglass. Bob Hollander, class member and new owner of the boat, can be seen paddling the Sea Bee at Pierce Lake.
