Davids Comments.....

"Yesterday I was finally able to get the Sonnet out into the open Atlantic. Launched at Stonington, Ct - paddle across Little Narragansett Bay - around
Napatree Point into the Atlantic off Watch Hill, Rhode Island. Use the (BACK) key to return.

The Sonnet continues to impress. The boat feels very solid in the Atlantic rolling swells, it is very easy to maneuver and surfs well when it catches a bit of a wave. I did a landing and launch again in the small surf on the beach at Napatree. The Sonnet is just fun to paddle, still very comfortable and relaxing, even in the larger swells."
