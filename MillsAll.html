David, from Staten Island, New York displays pics of his modified Sea Tour 15. He multiplied all dimensions by 1.2 to produce a longer and wider Sea Tour for fishing. The plywood deck plates are for rod holders (upper left). The skin is sewn nylon coated with urethane. David reports that the boat is fast, tracks and turns well, and has exceptional final stability. The Verazano bridge ( lower right) was the site of the initial launch.
