Mark 1" X 6" PVC tie-downs with pencil
Cut out tie-down with scissors. Clean with MEK and apply 3 coats of vinyl cement. Allow to dry completely.
Slide on 1" D-Rings and fold in half. Apply heat and pressure. Brush 3 coats of vinyl cement on one side of tie-down.


Mark tie-down locations with masking tape. Clean area with MEK and apply three coats of vinyl cement.
Position tie-downs and apply heat and pressure.
Attach deck rigging.
